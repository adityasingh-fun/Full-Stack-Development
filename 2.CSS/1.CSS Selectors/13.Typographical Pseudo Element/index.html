The Fascinating Origins of Coffee

Quick Facts

Coffee beans are actually the pit of a berry.

The word 'coffee' comes from a Arbaic word 'wine'.

Coffe is more than just a beverage; it's a culture, an economy, and a daily ritual for millions. The history of this captivating drink dates back centuries.

The tale of coffee begins in Ethopian Islands, where the legend claims the goat header Kaldi first discovered its magical properties.

The journey of coffee is as rich and complex as its brew. It's a story of royality, religion and revolution.
